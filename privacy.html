Privacy Policy

How BuildersArk LLC and QObserva collect, use, and protect information.

Last updated: January 2026

Operator: This Privacy Policy is issued by BuildersArk LLC (“we,” “us,” “our,” or “BuildersArk”). QObserva is a brand and open-source project of BuildersArk LLC. This policy applies to the website at qobserva.com, related online properties we operate, and (where stated) to your use of QObserva software and any future paid or cloud-based services we may offer.

1. Introduction and Scope

We are committed to transparency about the limited data we collect and how we use it. QObserva is offered first as an open-source, local-first observability tool for quantum computing workflows. Much of the software runs on your own systems and does not send your runtime data to us. This policy describes:

  • Information we collect when you visit our website or interact with us online
  • Information related to the open-source QObserva software and any future paid or cloud offerings
  • How we use, store, and share that information
  • Your choices and rights

By using our website or (where applicable) our services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our website or services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We may collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Contact us (e.g., via email or a contact form), including name, email address, and the content of your message
  • Subscribe to newsletters or notifications (if we offer them)
  • Create an account or sign up for a paid or cloud-based QObserva service (when and if we offer such services)
  • Participate in surveys, feedback, or community discussions we host

2.2 Information Collected Automatically (Website)

When you visit qobserva.com or our other sites, we or our service providers may automatically collect:

  • Log data: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, referring URLs, pages viewed, date and time of access, and similar technical data
  • Cookies and similar technologies: We may use cookies, local storage, or similar technologies for essential site operation, analytics (if we use them), and preferences. See Section 5 for more detail.

We do not currently use third-party advertising or tracking for marketing. If we introduce analytics, we will describe them here and, where required, offer opt-out or consent options.

2.3 Open-Source and Local-First Software

The core QObserva software is designed to run locally. By default, it does not transmit your quantum run data, metrics, or personally identifiable information to BuildersArk or any third party. If you use only the open-source, local deployment:

  • We do not collect or store your runtime data, algorithm outputs, or usage patterns from the software itself.
  • Any data processed by the software remains on your systems unless you explicitly configure it to send data elsewhere (e.g., to your own infrastructure).

If we later offer optional cloud features, paid plans, or telemetry, we will update this policy and, where required by law, obtain consent before collecting such data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our website and services

  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of BuildersArk, our users, or the public
  • Analyze usage of our website (if we use analytics) to improve content and experience
  • For any other purpose disclosed at the time of collection or with your consent

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for third-party advertising or profiling in ways that would identify you personally, except as described in this policy or with your consent.
